About Us

Team HandyKrafts

We are here to remove influence of distributors and middlemen, make artisans more profitable, increase the mobility of products and decrease the retail price significantly.

With the growth and outset of E-commerce platforms, buying and selling products have become quite easier and user-friendly but do all products receive equal opportunity, the answer is no, the concern is regarding handicrafts and handloom-based products that are mostly made in small scale industries and are situated in regions where sellers cannot directly connect to amazon, flipkart and other e-commerce platforms, as many a times they aren't serviceable in those regions. Hence, a lot of middlemen get involved in selling these products resulting in buying them from artisans at a low cost and selling them at an inflated price in the market. From our e-commerce platform we plan to directly sell these products from warehouses setup by us in those regions giving both the artisans and the buyers products at a justified price and removing all the middlemen like distributor, wholesaler, and retailer.
